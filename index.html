WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN GYM

Joining a gym provides a Structured Environment for Exercise , Joining a gym offers numerous benefits, including :

  • Increase Immune system

    exercise increases blood and lymph flow as your muscles contract, it also increases the circulation of immune cells, making them roam the body at a higher rate and higher numbers, said Dr. Nieman.

  • Increase Muscle Strength

    Muscular strength relates to your ability to move and lift objects. Its measured by how much force you can exert and how much weight you can lift for a short period of time.

  • Reduce stress

    Exercise is a form of physical stress. Can physical stress relieve mental stress? Alexander Pope thought so: "Strength of mind is exercise, not rest." Plato agreed: "Exercise would cure a guilty conscience."

  • Boost happy chemicals

    When you exercise, your body releases chemicals called endorphins. These endorphins interact with the receptors in your brain that reduce your perception of pain. Endorphins also trigger a positive feeling in the body.

  • Improve self-confidence

    physical confidence being one of them. Exercise is an important tool that helps us achieve this physical confidence. Beyond the postural imbalances, muscle tensions and day-to-day energy demands.

  • Enjoy the great outdoors

    Outdoor exercise can help you improve your mood, sleep quality, and energy levels. The fresh air also gives you a boost and makes it easier to focus on what you're doing.
